If you need to update your business details, like your business address, here’s how.

## **Change your sign in name**

1. In Kinde, go to the top right corner and select your avatar.
2. In the menu that opens, select **Account details**.
3. Make the changes you want and select **Save**.

## **Change business details**

1. In Kinde, go to **Settings > Business > Details.**
2. If you wan to change your business name, this will not impact your kinde business URL that was already created.
3. Update your primary email and phone.
4. Make any other changes and select **Save**.
